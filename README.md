# 🍕 Pizza Sales Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Overview

The Pizza Sales Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze sales performance and customer purchasing behavior. The dashboard converts raw sales data into actionable insights through dynamic visualizations, KPI tracking, and interactive filtering.

## 🎯 Project Objectives

* Monitor overall sales performance.
* Analyze revenue trends and order patterns.
* Identify best-selling pizza categories and products.
* Understand customer preferences by pizza size.
* Discover peak ordering periods.
* Support data-driven business decisions.

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* 💰 Total Revenue
* 📦 Total Orders
* 🍕 Total Pizzas Sold
* 📈 Average Order Value
* 🛒 Average Pizzas per Order

### Interactive Visualizations

* Revenue by Pizza Category
* Revenue by Pizza Size
* Top Performing Pizzas
* Sales Trends Over Time
* Orders by Day of Week
* Orders by Hour
* Quantity Sold Analysis

### Filters & Slicers

* Pizza Category
* Pizza Size
* Month
* Quarter

## 🛠️ Technologies Used

* Microsoft Power BI
* Power Query
* DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
* Data Modeling
* Business Intelligence & Data Visualization

## 📈 Key Insights

* Peak sales are concentrated during specific meal hours.
* Certain pizza categories contribute significantly to total revenue.
* A small number of products generate a large share of overall sales.
* Customer demand varies across weekdays and weekends.
* Pizza size preferences have a direct impact on revenue generation.

## 🚀 Business Impact

* Enables quick monitoring of business performance.
* Helps identify high-revenue products for promotion.
* Supports inventory and demand planning.
* Improves understanding of customer purchasing behavior.
* Facilitates data-driven decision-making through interactive reporting.
